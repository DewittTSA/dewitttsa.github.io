Global Warming?

﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿Global warming is a huge topic in the modern world. Our species, Homo Sapiens, is extremely dependent on fossil fuels for our electricity, which is what we use to heat our homes and power our cars, televisions, and more. Fossil fuels release very high amounts of CO2 into the Atmosphere, which is a factor that causes global warming.

Renewable energy is an effective way to deal with this issue. Renewable energy is a type of energy that comes from a source that will not run out. Most types of renewable energy are clean, meaning that they don't release as much CO2 into the atmosphere as fossil fuels.

Technology is helping to promote renewable energy sources. Technology and science combined creates new and more efficient ways to create electricity. Each accomplishment towards renewable energy is an important accomplishment for future generations of the human race.
