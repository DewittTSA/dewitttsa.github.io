Wind Energy

What is Wind Energy?

Wind Energy is energy harnessed through the use of windmills. When the wind blows the windmill, it turns a generator, which in turn produces electricity. Wind energy is one of the easiest forms of renewable energy to be stored and accessed. Windmills are usually built on plains and other flat lands.
Careers:
Careers pertaining to wind energy include:
  • Wind Turbine researcher/engineer
  • Windmill engineer
  • Windmill construction builder

How Technology is Incorporated into Wind Energy
There are many ways that new and innovative technologies are making Wind Energy increasingly efficient and effective. For example, Vortex has invented a wind turbine that takes advantage of wind vortexes, and as a result, doesn't rotate (it looks like an asparagus).
