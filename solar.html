Solar Energy

Solar Energy is energy gotten from the sun. It is collected by Solar Panels.

About Solar Energy

Solar energy is energy collected from the sun using solar panels. This energy source is renewable.
It has many advantages:
‣ It's quiet
‣ It's clean
‣ It's effective
It also has some disadvantages:
‣ Considered ugly
‣ Takes up space
‣ Only works when the sun is out
‣ Relatively expensive

​New technology is promoting Solar Energy in many ways. New technology is making Solar Energy a better choice than unclean energy and is helping to reduce the costs of solar panels. 
Many jobs have been created from solar energy. Some include:
  • Solar Panel Research and Development
  • Solar Panel Manufacturing and Engineering
  • Solar Panel Farm Manager
Here are some companies related to solar energy.
  • First Solar
  • SunMaxx Solar
  • Blue Pacific Solar
Solar Energy is a new industry and is always creating jobs. More jobs will be created as Solar Energy advances.
